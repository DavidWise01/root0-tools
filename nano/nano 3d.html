What am I looking at?

One TRIAD is three transistors: MERKLE (verify), YES (affirm), NO (negate). Together they answer: can I verify this, is it true, is it false?

At higher scales each TRIAD shrinks to a three-dot cluster. The whole pattern replicates fractally. Click any TRIAD to inspect.

What can go wrong?

HOLD: gate confirms the axiom. Computation proceeds.

VIOLATED: NO gate fired. Fault detected. Propagates along fault chains.

UNDECIDABLE: neither YES nor NO fired. Not enough evidence.

CONTRADICTION: both YES and NO fired. Logical impossibility. System halts.

UNVERIFIABLE: MERKLE failed. Governance impossible at this gate.
